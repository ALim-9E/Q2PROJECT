Adobo

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Wash and prepare the chicken thighs. Transfer them to a bowl and then add the soy sauce, vinegar and black pepper. Massage well and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat a pan (no oil). Arrange the chicken in the pan, with the skin side down. Sear them on both sides until light brown. Once brown, remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Using the same pan and oil, add the ginger, and garlic and saute them a little. Add the chicken back to the pan. Add the bay leaves, whole peppercorns and the reserved marinade. You may add some sugar if you like (optional).
  4. Add some water. Cover the pot and let it simmer for 25 to 30 minutes using low medium heat. Stir a bit to cook evenly. Continue cooking until the sauce is reduced and the fat starts to render. Turn the heat off.
  5. Transfer the chicken adobo to a serving plate. Enjoy this with a lot of hot steaming rice. Yum!
