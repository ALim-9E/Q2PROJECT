Sinabawang Corned Beef

Ingredients

  • 2 cans corned beef
  • 2 medium potatoes (cut into small cubes)
  • 2 small tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • oil for sauteing
  • Onion rings (optional)

Instructions

  1. Open the cans of corned beef. Set aside.
  2. Heat a little oil in a pan. Add the potato cubes and then saute until brown and tender. Transfer to a separate container and set aside for a while.
  3. Using the same pan, add the garlic and onions. Saute them for a few minutes until aromatic. Then, add the tomatoes. Gently crush them while sauteing to bring out the liquid. Add the corned beef and saute for a whole minute. Season with some salt and pepper. Add a cup of water and wait for it to boil.
  4. Once the sauce is boiling, add the sauteed potatoes back to the pan. Stir a little and let it cook for a few more minutes. You may add some onion rings if you like.
  5. When all the ingredients come together, turn the heat off and transfer the sinabawang corned beef to a serving bowl.
  6. Enjoy the sinabawang corned beef with a lot of hot steaming rice. Yum!
